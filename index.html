Gaia Molinaro

I am a Neuroscience BSc graduate at King's College London and Cognitive and Decision Sciences MSc student at UCL, where I am currently researching at Tali Sharot's Affective Brain Lab. I am also Director of Events and Welfare Officer at the UCL Behavioural Innovations Society, content creator at Testable, and Unexplored Neuroscience fellow. In fall 2021, I will join Anne Collins' Computational Cognitive Neuroscience lab at UC Berkeley, in preparation for a career in research and innovation. I am interested in human and artificial learning and decision-making processes, and how internal factors (such as goals, emotion and motivation) influence them.

I'm always happy to meet new people from all backgrounds. Send me an email at gaiamolinaro@berkeley.edu if you would like to connect, or take a look at my CV.
